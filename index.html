About Us

SakhshamHR, a one of its kind “One Stop Shop” for all the HR Solutions under one roof, started in May 2012 with the intent of helping out small and medium size companies to set up best practices in HR. A team of highly skilled and experienced hr people aligned together from half decade to serve as a one stop solution for all HR needs. Our leaders bring deep industry insight and hands-on involvement to every engagement. The idea of forming this end-to-end, one stop HR firm, had the genesis in the word “Sakhsham”.
